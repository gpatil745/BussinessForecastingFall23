Box.test is a statistical test that helps us see if theres a pattern of repeating values in a time series. It gives us information like the chi-squared statistic, degrees of freedom, and p-value.

plot.ts(residuals(auto_fit))

hist(auto_fit$residuals)

tsdiag(auto_fit)

The graph of standardized residuals indicates that most of them fall within the expected range, suggesting nothing unusual. However, a few outliers suggest that the ARIMA model might not fully capture all the patterns in the data from 2011 to 2020. The p-values for the Ljung-Box statistic are all higher than 1.0, indicating that the residuals dont show a pattern over time. In the ACF graph, the correlation coefficients at all time lags are within the expected range, meaning they are not statistically significant.
